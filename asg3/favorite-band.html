Flower Face

About

Flower Face is the stage name used by bedroom pop singer-songwriter Ruby McKinnon. She has released four albums on streaming services: Fever Dreams (2016), Baby Teeth (2018), The Shark in Your Water (2022), and Girl Prometheus.

She is based in Ontario, Canada.

Why She Is My Favorite

The Lyrics

Flower Face's lyrics are beautifully poetic, and just as haunting. The perfect example of her masterful lyricism is her song "Angela", which features lines such as "You walk like a miracle, bathing in starlight / Your voice burning holes in the frame / I danced in the parking lot, cried in the taxi home / 'Cause I still remember your name". Through these lyrics, Flower Face expertly conveys the feeling of longing and heartbreak.
