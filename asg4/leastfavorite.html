Least Favorite (Fictional) Person

My least favorite (fictional) person is Gentle Criminal from the anime My Hero Academia - not because he's a villain, but because he's really, really, really annoying. And also his character design is super ugly.

Better Villains

My Hero Academia is filled with way better villains than this guy, such as:

  • Stain, who also uses the Internet to spread his evil, but is scary instead of annoying.
  • Dabi, another villain who uses media to fuel an agenda, but has a better agenda than clout-chasing.
  • Toga Himiko, a villain whose story also revolves around romance, but her flirting with her love interests doesn't make me gag.

Worst Hits

The fact that critics love him so much just makes me hate Gentle Criminal even more. Not only does he suck, but everyone except me is blind to it.
