    Domain Names

    Potential Names

    I had already settled on INSPOtting for my website's name. The main goal of my domain name discussion was to find a good suffix. Some of the ones I looked at included:

    • inspotting.org - $7.48 per year
    • inspotting.design - $10.98 per year
    • inspotting.art - $2.98 per year

    Honary mentions go to inspotting.inc, which for some bizarre reason is worth $498 per year, with a senty-six percent discount. Alas, I am but a humble and broke college student, so it's not meant to be.

    A Conversation About Potential Names

    When talking with my classmate, the breadth of the conversation was centered around budgeting. It was a big priority for both of us to find a domain name that had a good look to it, and wasn't too unprofessional, while also choosing something affordable - i.e. not going with inspotting.inc! Not only is that a lot of money we don't have, but there's also more room for variety in picking one of the cheaper, weirder options.

    We also discussed the value of certain suffixes over others. A site such as inspotting.design does a very good job giving prospective visitors an idea of what the site is about. But on the flip side, inspotting.org looks more professional and trustworthy.

    In the end, the name I like best is inspotting.design! There's a lot of pressure with a ".org" that I'm not sure I'm ready for. Also, I love how it gives a sense of what the site is about.
