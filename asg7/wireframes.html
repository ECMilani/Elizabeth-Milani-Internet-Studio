Assignment 7: Wireframes

What are the advantages and disadvantages of sketching wireframes over wireframe software?

Advantages

More options with sketching than with software. Sketching also isn't kept just to the computer, so it can be done whenever and wherever. Sketching is also always free, whereas some of the good software can get expensive.

Disadvantages

More designers and products managers are cusing software, especially on the graphic design side of things. Software is also more focused and better for collaboration and integration with other tools.
