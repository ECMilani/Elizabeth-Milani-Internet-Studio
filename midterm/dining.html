Dining

First House Catering

Enjoy catered meals made by authentic First House skeletons, with only the freshest ingredients. On the menu is tea (the Heavenly Emperor's favorite) and coffee for drinks, and a variety of salads and seafood entrees to choose from.

Magnus & Abigail's

Want something a little more personal than what the First House has to offer? Why not try some Fifth House delicacies instead? Enjoy delicious charcuterie boards, steaks, and even ice cream, along with so much more!

Ninth House Style Eating

Want something you can enjoy on the go? Why not try Ninth House Style Eating?! The Ninth House are experts in packaged, easily-digestible foods that can be squeezed out of neat little tubes... perfect for eating while studying or running from terrifying eldritch bone beasts!
