Activities

Tasks to Complete on Your Journey to Lyctorhood

The Pool Room

This room serves as a training facility and sparring court for cavaliers to let their energy out! This room also contains a "chemical pit" currently in the process of being refurbished into a functioning swimming pool, courtesy of the Third House. Enjoy a dip or a spar, or just some socializing with your fellow houses.

Lab 2: Transferance / Winnowing

"It's the theorem from the trial room, [...] It's the complete methodology for transference--for the utilisation of a living soul."

Head down the facility to really put your necromancer-cavalier bond to the test with Lab Two! The necromancer will stand in one sealed chamber, while the cavalier stands in the other - the necromancer will have to borrow the cavalier's eyes to help the cavalier fight the monster in their chamber, as a cavalier alone could never hope to figure out how to defeat the thing. A great way to really get to know your cavalier better - get inside their head, if you will.

Solve the Mystery

Upon your arrival at Canaan House, you will notice a pattern to those who fall victim to the monster and are ultimately slain. Solve the mystery by deciphering who is behind the pattern, who is a target, and how the bodies died. Who has motive? Who has made enemies? Who wants to discover the secrets of Lyctorhood, and who would rather keep them hidden? Who doesn't love a good murder mystery?
